Payment Transfers

Course payments cannot be transferred from one student to another.

Non-native Trainees

Non-native English trainees must have a good command of both written and spoken English. An IELTS score of 6 (or equivalent) is generally required.

Refunds and Cancellations

If you are dissatisfied with the course, you have 14 days in which to change your mind, provided no assignments have been submitted for assessment. You can expect a full refund in these circumstances. The refund is usually issued within 14 days of your instruction and will be paid by the same method as the original payment. No refunds can be given after 14 days of the course payment for any reason, except in the circumstance when it is deemed necessary to advise a trainee of his or her unsuitability to continue, possibly due to work of an unacceptably low standard. In this case the trainee must have submitted at least the first 3 modules for assessment.

You will be assigned a personal tutor for the duration of the course. You will be able to contact your tutor through the online campus.

Receiving access to your course
