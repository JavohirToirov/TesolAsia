INTESOL Franchise and Agency Opportunities

We Are Looking For Partnerships!

Join An Established Network! Partner INTESOL Worldwide And Benefit From 26 Years Of Proven Success.

INTESOL is looking for franchisees and agents to establish partnerships worldwide.

TEFL teaching and training is a massive global industry that continues to grow year upon year.

Whether the global economy is booming or in recession the TEFL market remains strong. Quite simply, there is always a global demand to learn and teach English! This fact ensures a secure investment for anyone wanting to start a business in TEFL, with expansive opportunities for growth and development into a long and secure future.

INTESOL is this year celebrating 27 years in business. During this time we have gained an excellent reputation in the industry.

We currently have branches in a number of locations worldwide.

We are now looking to expand our franchise and agency network globally.

At this time we are particularly interested in discussing opportunities in the following countries, but will consider all locations where we do not currently have an existing agreement:

  • USA
  • Australia/New Zealand
  • Latin America (Central & South America)
  • South Korea

Benefits of partnering INTESOL

  • Low start up costs.
  • You will be joining a successful business with brand recognition built over the last 26 years. This means your business will be instantly recognizable and trusted as a quality course provider, cutting out a great deal of the initial time it takes to become established and trusted.
  • All of our courses are accredited by ALAP, a specialist UK based ELT awarding organisation. This is a huge selling point when attracting students in an industry flooded with non reputable course providers.
  • All course materials are delivered in an easy to use online format, making grading and administration of the course simple and easy for both online and onsite courses.
  • Inclusion in centralised marketing campaigns at INTESOL head office that benefit the whole INTESOL group.
  • INTESOL does not charge large fees and then leave you to your own devices. We are committed to your long term success. We charge a small upfront fee and operate a royalty system. This expresses our commitment to your ongoing success and a mutually beneficial partnership.
