About INTESOL Worldwide

Our Mission

The overall mission of INTESOL is to help people from a broad range of backgrounds achieve a varied and fulfilling lifestyle through teaching English and living abroad.

Our aim is to help you develop and grow as a person by showing you how to become a confident, well-resourced, professional ESOL/EFL teacher.

Through continuing research and development INTESOL seeks to be the world leader in quality TESOL/TEFL training; TESOL/TEFL training that takes into account the demands of everyday life.

We are ranked amongst the top TEFL/TESOL course providers for the quality of our teaching so, whatever your reason for studying, you’ll find yourself in expert hands.

When you choose INTESOL Worldwide YOU CHOOSE:
  • An externally validated TEFL/TESOL Certification that is recognised worldwide.
  • To study with a well known TEFL/TESOL training provider, established since 1993.
  • Flexibility! With INTESOL you can earn your accredited TESOL/TEFL certification online, in-class or a combination of both.
  • All TEFL and TESOL courses are accredited by ALAP, a UK based awarding organisation.
  • Teaching practice included with selected online courses, giving you maximum flexibility to gain practical experience.
  • You gain access to our network of TEFL/TESOL job contacts around the world.
  • A course provider who will help with your visa – click here.

More About INTESOL Worldwide

INTESOL Worldwide was founded in the UK in 1993 in the belief that anyone possessing the passion to teach, the skills to express themselves clearly, and the desire to make a difference in the lives of others should be given the opportunity and encouragement to gain a professional certification in TESOL.

Today, INTESOL has accumulated over 26 years of quality course provision, and a proven track record as a renowned and respected accredited TEFL/TESOL course provider.
