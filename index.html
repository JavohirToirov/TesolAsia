120 Hour Certificate in TEFL Plus

New course acceptable worldwide for ANY type of teaching:-

  • In-class (also called onsite)
  • Online (from the comfort of your own home or in-company)
  • Teaching Young Learners – online or onsite

Includes:

  1. All training online with a personal tutor qualified to Masters in TESOL as a minimum
  2. Graded 120 hour Certificate in TEFL Plus direct from the Accrediting Body, ALAP
  3. Issue number verifiable on the ALAP website from anywhere in the world
  4. Course certificate and transcript in PDF format
  5. Hard copy with extra security of a hologram on request
  6. Interview with a world-leading online recruitment company if required (terms apply)

EXTRA – introduction to an Apostille Service with a discounted fee if your employer requires this.
