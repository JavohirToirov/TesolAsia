INTESOL Worldwide is registered under the UKRLP (UK Register of Learning Providers). Registration on the UKRLP means that we have been verified against a recognised legal source.

One of the most common questions we get asked is what the teaching job prospects are like for graduates of the INTESOL course and what the likelihood is of gaining employment with a reputable school. We are very proud to say that in over 27 years of running INTESOL courses, all of our graduates have gone on to secure teaching jobs. Not a single graduate was unable to find a job after completing one of our courses.
