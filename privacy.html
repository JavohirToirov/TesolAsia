Privacy Policy

Since 1993 INTESOL Worldwide Ltd has been dedicated to maintaining the highest standards in accredited TESOL/TEFL training.

What INTESOL Worldwide does with Personal Information

When you visit our website personal information is collected when voluntarily submitted by you by sending us an email. We will use this personal information including your IP (Internet Protocol) obtained from the site for system administration, allowing you to enter the site time and time again. In order to continually improve our service to you and for operational and quality assurance, we monitor web ‘traffic’, maintain log files of users’ access to site files and keep track of the domains from which students visit us so that we can take note of whether and how you use the information and services that we provide so that we can better tailor our content to your needs. We analyse this for trends and statistics and therefore may maintain this information for future use. The information INTESOL receives from or about you is stored on systems designed to prevent loss, misuse, unauthorised access, modification, disclosure or alteration.

Please note INTESOL does not store credit card details from our clients.

INTESOL Worldwide does not use cookies for collecting user information from the site. (A cookie is a small file sorted on a computer by a site with the objective to identify prior users of a site and to personalise your visit.)

We may use your information to offer you other associated INTESOL Worldwide services and keep you up to date with any INTESOL services that we think you may find valuable. If you do not wish to receive this information, please send an email to enquiries@intesoltesoltraining.com with ‘unsubscribe’ as the subject heading. We may provide your personal data to third parties as required by you, the government or law and for any other purpose deemed necessary.

What rights you have over your data

If you have an account on this site, or have left comments, you can request to receive an exported file of the personal data we hold about you, including any data you have provided to us. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you. This does not include any data we are obliged to keep for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

Notification of Changes

INTESOL Worldwide reserves the right to make changes to this statement at any time by posting revisions on the site. You are responsible for periodically checking our site for changes to this Privacy Statement. You may opt out of any posted change to our collection, use or disclosure of your personal information by sending an email to enquiries@intesoltesoltraining.com/p>

Access to Personal Information

In accordance with the Data Protection Act 1998, you are entitled to request a copy of the information we hold about you. We charge a fee for complying with this request.
